About

Buffalo Trail Entertainment has been creating content script-to-screen since 2015. We've combined our teams in Albuquerque and Los Angeles to provide the most exclusive production services. We specialize in A-list and international productions, high-minded content, partnering our top-rated production partners with hand-selected creative intellectual properties. We cultivate your creative vision to bring your stories to life!
